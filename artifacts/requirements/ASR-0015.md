# ASR-0015

**Priority:** 5300  
**Status:** Active  
**Created:** 2026-04-20  
**Updated:** 2026-04-20  

## Requirement

Every piece of domain metadata must have exactly one authoritative type — no second type may redeclare the same fields, even under different names. Violation produces naming drift, type mismatches, and silent breakage when the copies diverge.

## Notes

The implementation mechanism is composition (ADR-028): complex types hold an instance of the simpler type rather than copying its fields. This ASR captures the quality constraint that motivates that decision. Applies within layers, not just across them.
